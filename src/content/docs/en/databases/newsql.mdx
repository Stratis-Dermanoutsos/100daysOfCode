A ***NewSQL*** database, like ***SurrealDB***, is a type of database system that seeks to combine the benefits of both traditional ***SQL*** databases and newer ***NoSQL*** databases.
***NewSQL*** databases are designed to handle the scalability and performance challenges of modern applications while maintaining the familiar ***SQL*** query language and transactional consistency of traditional databases.
